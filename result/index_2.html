Насчет конкорда.

 
Нашел несколько интересных цитат на оффоруме(оригинал сообщения разработчика пока не нашел, к сожалению).
 
Спойлер
"To summarize what both the Dev and GM said: no, its not "illegal" to shoot/attract Concord as long as the player sucks up the consequences. But to do so with a throwaway alt (in this case, a toon that is created with the intention of shooting Concord and then to be biomassed later) is considered an exploit."

 

"Summoning Concorde is not the problem, the problem is avoiding security status penalties by biomassing characters and creating others to do the job."

 

CCP Atropus "Ah I see the misunderstanding; I was attempting to state that if you use an alt for committing illegal acts (illegal in the sense that CONCORD kicks your ass for it) and then recycle them as a method to avoid the repercussions, you are committing an exploit. It's the avoidance of these penalties that is the problem.
 
I hope this clarifies my earlier statement somewhat."

 

Другими словами, мы получаем по голове в том случае, если для суммона конкорда используются специально созданные персы, которые затем отправляются в биореактор.

 

P.S. CCP вообще не одобряет подобный подход к созданию персов.
